Nevermore DEX

+

Fixed-income yield curve for stablecoins on Solana. T-bill mechanics, on-chain.

Nevermore applies traditional fixed-income term structure to DeFi stablecoin liquidity. Liquidity providers lock USDC into 1, 5, or 20-day fixed-term positions and earn guaranteed annualized yields — longer commitments earn higher rates, exactly as a yield curve would predict. The protocol uses a DCF model to price each term's yield, creating an interest rate term structure modeled on fixed-income principles where yield increases with duration.

Rust · Solana · Built solo

Optus DEX

+

DEX aggregator on TON with best-execution routing, gasless transactions, and on-chain analytics.

Optus aggregates liquidity across decentralized exchanges on the TON blockchain, routing orders to deliver best-execution pricing and minimize slippage. Large orders are split across multiple venues to maximize price efficiency — similar to smart order routing in traditional markets. The protocol removes gas friction, making execution seamless for any trader type. Built above the DEX layer, Optus includes an analytics dashboard tracking live liquidity pool positions, trading volume, and on-chain activity across the TON network.
